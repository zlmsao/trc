Torch-network

is a platform which combines Lending-as-a-Service (LaaS)、Liquidity Pool-as-a-Service (LPaaS) with Staking-as-a-Service (SaaS).



Decentralized Finance is one of the most quickly growing innovations in financial services. Torch aims to create an open financial system to everyone which don’t need to rely on central authorities.

About

Torch adopt the best features of other protocol while providing its own unique features, enabling users to enjoy the promise of decentralized finance.

Lenders and borrowers can lend or borrow crypto assets by the anonymous way without registration or KYC.(inclube unsecured loan)

Investors, traders, and speculators can offer their idle capitals as custom pools with a fixed rate for lending.

Liquidity miners can provide assets to existing asset pools with an annual percentage return rate of up to 115% and manage Torch DAO by the vote of token .

Users can also stake PoS-based assets in existing pools to earn staking rewards according to the underlying protocols.

In addition, the governance token of Torch has a transfer burning mechanism, and each transfer has a relatively high probability burning 3% of the transfer amount.(Of course, if you're lucky, you might get more Toc.：)

ECOSYSTEM

LaaS Decentralized Loans

On our peer to peer loan marketplace a smart liquidity engine will match orders between borrowers and lenders, with algorithmically derived interest rates based on supply and demand. The matching engine can be adjusted to your needs or even set into a total manual mode where only your requirements count.

LPaaS Yield-Farming

Liquidity Provider can provide assets to existing pools and farm the Governance Token TOC with an annual percentage yield of up to 115%.

PaaS Custom-Pools

Pool as a service. User can create custom pools with a fixed rate for lending. To set up a pool the Governance Token TOC – is needed.

SaaS – Staking

Users can stake PoS-based assets in existing pools to earn staking rewards according to the underlying protocols. Major PoS assets like Cosmos, Tezos, Polkadot, Cardano, Kusama and Celo will be added.

WhitePaper

The Torch network aims to innovate DeFi through blockchain-based credit loans (including small loans and unsecured loans), thereby improving global access and gaining consumer trust, making DeFi financial solutions available to most users, and further Expand the user base of DeFi services.

Users will be able to borrow relatively little collateral on Torch, and enjoy borrowing or even unsecured loans.TORCH is based on the Ethereum blockchain network, which ensures security and reliability, while providing unlimited scalability.

Due to credit checks and third-party interference, centralized banking may also be inaccessible.Using Torch and Ethereum eliminates the need for a third party to participate, thus simplifying investment and financial management and making it more private.

Torch network is a reinterpreted DeFi protocol that specifically provides users with various loan services, such as small loans and unsecured loan services, so that DeFi services can basically be used by all users. In addition, Torch also supports the tokenization of assets and derivatives (such as CDS, etc.).

There is no financial institution to monitor personal finances and expenditures, but to establish smart contracts. Smart contracts are contracts encrypted into the blockchain, and assets will be released to the recipient in real time.
